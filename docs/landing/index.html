Why Skerry

Everything you need to run a creator community

The UX you love, on infrastructure you control.

💬

Discord-like UX

Servers, channels, threads, reactions, pinned messages. Everything your community already knows how to use.

🌐

Matrix Federation

Built on the Matrix protocol. Users on different Skerry instances can interact. No single point of control.

🔗

Discord Bridge

Bridge Skerry channels to Discord. Messages flow both ways. Your community stays connected wherever they prefer to be.

🎙️

Voice & Video

Built-in voice rooms with WebRTC via LiveKit. Hop in, share your screen, stream — no third-party services required.

🛡️

Scoped Moderation

Hub admins, space owners, moderators. Granular role-based permissions at every level. Audit log tracks everything.

🔒

Data Sovereignty

Your data lives on your server. End-to-end encryption via Matrix. No telemetry, no tracking, no ads.
